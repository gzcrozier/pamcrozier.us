  • Focus on promoting and growing our local economy
  • Ensure that our first responders have all the resources they need to keep us safe

By never losing sight of these things, as well as my passion for our community, the love of my family, and my faith in God, I aim to help the District 67 become an even better, safer, and freer place to live and raise a family.
